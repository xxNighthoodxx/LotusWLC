About Us

Since 1991, we started with a lunch box as a hobby and for the requirements of the requested family members and friends, when the demand for the product raised due to your good service more people requested for the requirements and we expanded with the help of V-Guard products and been in the feel for a long time with service and installation and then V-Guard stopped its water level segment so we had a support set of the service request to keep the service of saving water in the stream, then in 2013 Lotus Water Level Controllers (Lotus WLC) came to existence, Even those there was a lot of water level controllers in the market just keeping the profit in the view, Lotus WLC stud out of the game to help people to safe water and electricity, the technology which is been used in the Lotus Systems saves 80% consumption of power compared to other controllers.

Lotus WLC's vision is to bring clean and stability of energy and saving nature at the same time, Using high-quality materials also gives long life to the water (pump) motor and Lotus system.
